Smart Desert City

Urban Innovation for New Mansoura

Vision & Concept

The Smart Desert City is a visionary urban planning project aimed at transforming arid landscapes into sustainable, technology-driven communities. Proposed for the expansion of New Mansoura, this concept focuses on harmony between nature and high-tech infrastructure.

By utilizing solar energy, advanced water recycling systems, and IoT-enabled public services, the city aims to provide a high quality of life while maintaining a minimal environmental footprint.

Smart Infrastructure

Civil Engineering

Designing resilient structural and infrastructure systems for harsh desert environments by addressing extreme heat, strong winds, and sand movement.

This involves using durable materials, climate-adapted construction methods, and sustainable infrastructure for water, energy, and transportation to ensure long-term efficiency and sustainability.

Mechanical Engineering

Developing efficient cooling, ventilation, and mechanical systems to maintain comfortable indoor conditions, enhance energy efficiency and improve equipment performance through innovative temperature control, airflow management, and sustainable design solutions.

Mechatronics Engineering

Integrating smart sensors, automation, and centralized control to enable real-time monitoring and analysis, enhancing system performance, operational efficiency, safety, and sustainability within facilities.

Aerospace Engineering

Designing and implementing drone airports and integrated air systems with planned infrastructure, runways, and automated air traffic management to enable safe, efficient drone operations across diverse environments.

Petroleum & Gas Engineering

Designing energy extraction and conversion systems using natural gas to enhance production efficiency, ensure resource sustainability, and reduce emissions through advanced technologies for safe and efficient generation of electrical and thermal energy.
